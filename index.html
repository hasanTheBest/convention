About Us

Awarding Wining

The mission of the Orange County Convention Center is economic development. By hosting regional, national and international conventions.

Meetings and trade shows, the convention center infuses the local economy with new money and expanding business opportunities.

Service Providing

The award-winning Orange County Convention Center provides approximately $3 billion in economic impact to Central Florida annually. In recent years.

The Convention Center has averaged nearly 200 events, including 115 conventions and tradeshows that attract more than 1.5 million attendees to the region each year.
